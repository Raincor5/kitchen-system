Key Features

Streamline your kitchen operations with our integrated system

Label Generation & Printing

Create and print custom labels with QR codes for food items, enabling digital tracking throughout the food lifecycle.

Food Traceability

Track food items from preparation to service, ensuring compliance with food safety regulations and reducing waste.

Recipe Management

Manage, scale, and optimize recipes for various portion sizes with automatic unit conversion and ingredient adjustments.

Prep Tracking

Monitor food preparation processes in real-time with interactive drag-and-drop interface and status updates.

Storage Organization

Optimize kitchen storage with digital gastronorm tray management and inventory tracking.

Mobile Access

Access all features on-the-go with mobile applications designed for kitchen environments.

System Components

Our integrated system consists of several specialized components

Kitchen Manager

The central component responsible for label generation, printing, and integration with kitchen equipment.

  • QR code generation for digital tracking
  • Custom label design and printing
  • Integration with label printers
API Documentation

Food Traceability Tracker (FTT)

Android application for label printing and scanning to track food items throughout their lifecycle.

  • Scan labels to track food items
  • Print labels directly from mobile devices
  • Real-time updates on food status

Prep Tracker

Mobile application for tracking food preparation processes in real-time with interactive features.

  • Interactive drag-and-drop interface
  • Real-time preparation status
  • Digital inventory management

Recipe Upscaler

Recipe management system with web and mobile applications for scaling and optimizing recipes.

  • Automatic unit conversion
  • Portion scaling with ingredient adjustments
  • Recipe sharing and export
